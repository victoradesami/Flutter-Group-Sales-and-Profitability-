# Flutter-Group-Sales-and-Profitability-
Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard designed to provide business stakeholders with a comprehensive view of sales performance, revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and regional insights. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visualizations that support data-driven decision-making.

# Objectives
Monitor overall sales performance
Identify top-performing products and categories
Analyze regional sales distribution
Discover top revenue-generating cities
Track product quantities sold
Highlight key business insights for better decision-making

# Business Insights

The dashboard includes narrative insights to explain trends, such as:

Beverages generate the highest revenue.
East region contributes the largest share of sales.
New York is the highest revenue-generating city.
Salesperson performance varies across product categories.
Certain categories present opportunities for sales improvement.
